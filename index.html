I'm a Postdoctoral Associate at Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Minnesota in Minnesota, where I mostly works on Energy-Efficient Logic and Memory and Machine-learning accelerated EDA.

I graduated from IIT Kanpur, Kanpur, where I was advised by Yogesh S. Chauhan. At IIT Kanpur, I mostly worked on the Energy-Efficient Nanoscale Transistors and Compact Modeling of Electron Devices.

My research covers a broad spectrum, ranging from the core principles to practical implementations in electron devices and materials.

It’s a fusion of cutting-edge futuristic concepts with practical applications in the present day.
